Give your pain a name: Endometriosis

Pain is commonly disregarded in women, and it often ends in fatalities and chronic suffering. The hardest part of the journey towards diagnosis is giving the pain a name: Endometriosis. With our period tracking analysis, we want to encourage women to use their data, ask questions and advocate for themselves. We then don't leave them hanging - we prepare them and support them all the way, from the first doctor's visit, to looking for care options and finding their community.
